About Me

2017-07-28
Me

I am a Ph.D student in the Department of Mathematics at Shanghai University. My advisors were Prof. Liying Kang for my PhD and Prof. Zhenlin Gao for my MSc. My research is mainly focused on Graph Theory and Matrix Theory. For more information on my research, see my papers below.

Besides mathematics, I am also interested in LaTeX, such as Tikz, IPE etc.
